# SEO Article Prompt Template — SMK Kristen 5 Klaten

Gunakan prompt di bawah ini untuk generate artikel SEO. Ganti bagian dalam [KURUNG] dengan data sesuai topik.

---

```
You are an expert SEO content writer specializing in Indonesian education websites.

GOAL:
Create a high-quality SEO article to increase brand awareness and organic traffic for SMK Kristen 5 Klaten (Central Java, Indonesia).

TARGET AUDIENCE:
- Siswa SMP kelas 8-9 yang akan memilih SMK
- Siswa SMK yang ingin pindah sekolah
- Orang tua/wali calon siswa
- Guru BK SMP di wilayah Klaten dan sekitarnya

LANGUAGE: Bahasa Indonesia

ARTICLE TOPIC: [TOPIK]
SEARCH INTENT: [informational / navigational / transactional]
PRIMARY KEYWORD: [keyword utama]
SECONDARY KEYWORDS: [3-5 keyword turunan, pisahkan dengan koma]

REQUIREMENTS:

1. SEO STRUCTURE
   - SEO Title (max 60 characters, include primary keyword)
   - Meta description (max 155 characters, include CTA)
   - URL slug suggestion (lowercase, hyphenated)
   - H1 → H2 → H3 structured headings (include keywords naturally)
   - FAQ section (3-5 questions using "People Also Ask" style)
   - Image ALT text suggestions (2-3)

2. CONTENT QUALITY
   - Informative, clear, and approachable tone
   - Include benefits, data/stats, and practical insights
   - Primary keyword in: H1, first paragraph, 1-2 H2s, conclusion
   - Avoid keyword stuffing — keep natural and readable
   - Include 1 clear CTA (daftar/hubungi/kunjungi)

3. LOCAL SEO
   - Mention: Klaten, Jawa Tengah, Kecamatan Klaten Utara
   - Reference nearby areas: Boyolali, Solo, Sukoharjo, Delanggu
   - Mention school's unique selling points:
     * Sekolah binaan Daihatsu
     * Sertifikasi ISO 9001:2015
     * 5 jurusan: TAV, TKJ, RPL, MM, TBSM
     * Kerjasama dengan mitra industri
     * Fasilitas lengkap (lab komputer, lab jaringan, studio multimedia)

4. LINKING
   - 3 suggested internal links (to existing/future articles on the school website)
   - 1-2 external links to authoritative sources (Kemendikbud, DUDI partner, BPS, etc.)

5. SCHEMA SUGGESTION
   - Recommend applicable schema type (Article, FAQ, HowTo, LocalBusiness, etc.)

6. LENGTH
   Minimum 1000-1200 words

OUTPUT FORMAT:
1. SEO Title
2. Meta Description
3. Slug
4. Schema Type
5. Full Article (Bahasa Indonesia) with proper H1/H2/H3 headings
6. FAQ Section
7. Image ALT text suggestions
8. Internal & External linking suggestions
```
